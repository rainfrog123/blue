# Mean Reversion of the "Micro-Trend"

On a 5s chart, price often overextends due to a single large market order clearing the book.

## Logic

When price moves X standard deviations away from the **VWAP (Volume Weighted Average Price)** in under 15 seconds, it often snaps back as liquidity providers "refill" the gap.

## Tool

Use **Bollinger Bands** set to a high period (e.g., 200) on the 5s timeframe to identify these "liquidity gaps."
